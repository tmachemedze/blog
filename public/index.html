Reapportion spatial overlaps between South African census boundaries: 2001 and 2011

06 Dec 2018, 00:00

Introduction The documents accompanying the South African census of 2011 state that demarcations of geographical boundaries underwent a number of changes at provincial and municipal boundaries relative to those used for the 2001 census. This make it difficult to compare the 2011 census and the preceding census at this level. The aim of this exercise is to determine what proportion of the preceding census boundaries are included in the boundaries of the latest census.
